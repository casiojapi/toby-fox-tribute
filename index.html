Toby Fox

toby playing the pianito.
  • Toby "Radiation" Fox (born October 11, 1991), also known as FwugRadiation, is a music composer and game developer. He developed the critically acclaimed RPG Undertale and produced its soundtrack in 2015. His avatar is the Annoying Dog.

  • He studied environmental science at Northeastern University in Boston, Massachusetts.

  • Started composing a variety of music for Andrew Hussie's 2009 webcomic Homestuck during his senior year of high school.

  • Froggit (/ˈfrɒgᵻt/) is a species of monster the protagonist encounters in the Ruins. Froggits also appear as NPCs throughout the Ruins. Its Hard Mode and CORE counterpart is Final Froggit.

  • Whimsun (/ˈhwɪmzən/) is a monster encountered in the Ruins. Its Hard Mode and CORE counterpart is Whimsalot.

  • REALLY NOT FEELIN UP TO IT RIGHT NOW. SORRY. Napstablook (/ˈnæpstəˌbluːk/), known in the UnderNet as NAPSTABLOOK22, is a melancholic ghost monster and musician/DJ who lives in Waterfall, first seen and encountered in the Ruins as the game's first miniboss.

  • Vegetoid (/ˈvɛdʒɪˌtɔɪd/) is a species of monster encountered in the Ruins. Its Hard Mode counterpart is Parsnik.
